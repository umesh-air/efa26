About Integricate

I am Umesh Air from Nepal, the creator of Integricate, a project focused on unmanned aerial systems software. As a young innovator, I admire how Europe encourages founders and builders to develop original ideas instead of simply copying existing solutions. This culture of creativity, collaboration, and long-term thinking is one of the reasons Europe continues to succeed in technology and innovation.

Europe's Startup Ecosystem

This website aims to make Europe's startup ecosystem more accessible to young founders and early-stage innovators. It provides information about European startup incubators, guidance on how to write effective cold emails to connect with them, and a curated list of events across Europe that promote entrepreneurship and technological innovation.
