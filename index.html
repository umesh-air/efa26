How Europe Is Shaping the Future of Technology and Innovation

Europe’s success in technology is driven by a strong culture of innovation and a supportive ecosystem for startups. Across the continent, governments, universities, incubators, and industry leaders work together to encourage entrepreneurs to transform creative ideas into real solutions. This environment provides startups with access to mentorship, funding opportunities, and collaborative networks that help them grow and succeed. I am grateful for the opportunity to apply to the European Forum Alpbach 2026 with my own creative project. I developed a website that gathers information about startup incubators and innovation events across Europe. The platform is designed to help new founders easily discover resources, support programs, and events that can guide them in building and developing their startups.
